DTRACKER

DTRACKER is a waste management app designed to streamline residential waste disposal and collection. Our innovative technology connects homes, individuals, and businesses with waste disposal agents, addressing the significant challenge of waste disposal in the Cameroonian community.

SWIMS platform is software we built for our users that benefits both dustbin users and waste collectors by providing features going from real time waste to monitoring, smart collection, plan generation, routing optimization

SWIMSENSE
Version 1.0

A fast implantable dustbin sensor with WiFi capabilties to communicate with the swims cloud. This sensor has the full capability to monitor the level of waste in dustbins and give realtime updates to the SWIMS platform.
